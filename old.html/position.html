Open positions

Several positions (bioinformatics research assistant, programmer, and PhD studentship in bioinformatics) are available in our lab at the Department of Chemical Pathology of The Chinese University of Hong Kong.

The candidate's tasks include the implementation of software for the next generation sequencing data analysis including but not limited to ChIP-seq, RNA-seq, Methylation-seq, and whole genome sequencing since our research projects are tightly integrated with the current research related to the gene regulation in muscle proliferation and differentiation (myogenesis) and molecular diagnosis of disease using next generation sequencing technologies.

Requirements

The successful candidate should:
  • hold a bachelor or master degree in computer science, electrical engineering or biology;
  • skilled in the following programming languages: (Perl|Python) & (C|C++|Java) & R.

How to apply

Interested applicants should submit a letter of interest with CV and two references (including name and contact information) to haosun _AT_ cuhk dot edu dot hk with subject line "Application for positions in Sun Lab".
